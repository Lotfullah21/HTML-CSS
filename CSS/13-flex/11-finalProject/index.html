About us

Empowering Afghan girls

We are dedicated to empowering Afghan girls by providing them with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in the fields of computer science, programming, and machine learning. Our mission is to create a supportive and inspiring environment where they can learn, grow, and achieve their dreams.

Join us on this journey and be a part of a community that values education, innovation, and the future of young Afghan women.
